What’s Happening

Defence budgets in the US, Australia and AUKUS partners are climbing, but local content rules and export‑control paperwork are getting tighter.

What HR/Leadership Should Do
  • Hire/retain compliance specialists and ITAR‑savvy program managers.
  • Grow an Australian engineering base so bids score higher on “sovereign capability.”
What’s Happening

We’re well‑funded (>US $1bn raised) but interest rates are high and customers want proven results before signing big, multi‑year deals.

What HR/Leadership Should Do
  • Prioritise roles that turn pilots into production contracts (BD engineers, sustainment teams).
  • Keep tight controls on burn until revenue ramps.
What’s Happening

People worry about “killer robots.” Talent - especially engineers - want to see a clear, ethical mission.

What HR/Leadership Should Do
  • Launch and publicise a “Responsible Autonomy” charter; make ethics training part of onboarding.
  • Highlight our purpose (“protect service members & civilians”) in recruitment marketing.
What’s Happening

Our Hivemind autonomy software is a big differentiator, but it needs constant R&D and secure chip supply.

What HR/Leadership Should Do
  • Invest in continuous‑learning budgets and clear career paths for AI talent.
  • Build partnerships with local suppliers to buffer any chip or export disruptions.
What’s Happening

New rules (DoD 3000.09, Australian DSTG guidance) demand explainable, well‑tested AI. Clearance timelines can stall hiring.

What HR/Leadership Should Do
  • Adopt “clear‑to‑hire” recruiting (start security vetting before day 1).
  • Set up an ethics & safety board to show regulators - and staff - we’re serious.
What’s Happening

Drones save fuel compared with manned aircraft, but battery waste and ESG reporting are becoming procurement check‑boxes.

What HR/Leadership Should Do
  • Add an ESG lead to track e‑waste and sustainable sourcing.
  • Include “green operations” metrics in supplier audits.

Proposed Solution: A Scalable Onboarding Platform

Automate at Source

A single source of truth for all data, documents, and compliance artifacts.

Compliance-by-Default

Embed Fair Work, NES checks, and security clearances into automated workflows.

Data-Led Operations

Instrument the platform with KPIs like Time-to-Hire and Onboarding CSAT.

The 90-Day Execution Plan

[ Phase 1: Discover & Stabilise (Days 1-30) ]

  • Gap-analysis workshop with US People Ops; map existing vs AU legal needs.
  • Draft "AU Onboarding Compliance Checklist" (FWIS, Closing Loopholes, etc.).
  • Select SaaS onboarding platform & sign local data-processing addendum.
  • ↳ Onboarding Platform Analysis

[ Phase 2: Build & Pilot (Days 31-60) ]

  • Design workflow incl. e-contracts, TFN declaration, super choice, and security vetting.
  • Integrate HRIS→Okta→IT ticketing for Day-1 access.
  • Onboard first test cohort 100% through the new platform.

[ Phase 3: Launch & Optimise (Days 61-90) ]

  • Full production launch for all new Australian hires.
  • Go-live with data dashboards tracking key success metrics.
  • Establish continuous-improvement backlog with US People Ops.
