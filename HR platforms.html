Platform Deep Dive

BambooHR

All-in-one HRIS for SMBs

A lightweight and quick-to-implement solution. Its key strength for Shield AI APAC lies in its strong Australian integrations with platforms like Xero and MYOB, plus available localisation add-ons.

~A$14.00 PEPM

Rippling

Unified HR + IT + Finance

Automates new hire provisioning for both HR (onboarding) and IT (hardware, apps). This makes it highly scalable across multiple APAC entities and provides strong control for offboarding risk.

~A$11.20+ PEPM

Sapling (Kallidus)

Purpose-built Onboarding

A strong contender if the primary goal is a best-of-breed onboarding "layer" to work on top of an existing core HR system (like UKG UltiPro). Ideal for distributed teams.

~A$9.40 PEPM

Enboarder

Engagement-led Onboarding

Sydney-founded, it delivers a premium, "journey-led" candidate experience using nudges and SMS. Offers strong Australian compliance but comes at a premium price point.

~A$19.30 PEPM

Deel

Global EOR & Payroll

Provides the fastest path into new markets like Taiwan or South Korea without establishing local entities. The core HRIS is free, you only pay for the high-cost EOR seats you use.

EOR Seat Cost

~A$838 PEPM

HiBob (Bob)

Modern Mid-Market HRIS

Offers rich analytics and robust workflows with strong onboarding features. It has a significant APAC customer base but comes at a higher unit cost than competitors like BambooHR or Rippling.

~A$22-35 PEPM
